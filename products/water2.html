How does the Naiade work?
Water from a well or surface source such as a river,lake,reservoir or stream is poured into the unit either by hand or via piping. Filters remove impurities carried in the water including nematodes. The ultraviolet light renders all viruses, bacteria, protozoa and worm eggs harmless.
Water only produced if system works correctly
Water will only flow out of the Naiade if it is functioning correctly. If the system is not functioning correctly the water flow is halted and the unit shuts down. This will happen for instance if the filters become blocked or if there is insufficient sunlight being absorbed by the solar panel.
Electrical energy
Normally the Naiade works as an independent unit. The solar panel absorbs sunlight which is converted to energy to power the ultraviolet light. However it isalso possible to operate the Naiade without using the solar panel if a source of electricity is available.
Simple maintenance
The daily maintenance of the Naiade consists of cleaning the filters. This can easily be done by hand. In addition the ultraviolet lamp needs to be checked and replanced on a regular basis.
