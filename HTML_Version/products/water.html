Shortage of clean drinking water
For millions of people a shortage of clean drinking water is a reality they must face every day. Sometimes they simply do not have enough water, in other cases the water that is available is contaminated.
Dirty water is a health hazard
In developing countries poor water quailty is the leading cause of health problems. Eighty percent of all illnesses in developing countries are cauesd by polluted water. It is estimated that two million people die from these illnesses every year.
